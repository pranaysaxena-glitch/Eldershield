How It Works

From Fall to Family Alert in Under 15 Seconds

ElderShield uses a two-stage detection algorithm — no false alarms, no missed events.

1

Wear It

Clip ElderShield to your parent's belt or waist. The built-in sensor monitors movement 24/7 — no buttons, no setup needed each day.

2

Fall Detected

The MPU6050 sensor detects a sudden impact spike above 2.5g followed by stillness — confirming a real fall, not just a bump or quick sit-down.

3

Instant Alert

The device immediately sounds a local buzzer and sends an SMS to your registered phone number. No internet required — works on any SIM network.

4

You Respond

You receive the SMS within 15 seconds and can call back, dispatch help, or contact emergency services — all before the situation gets worse.

FAQ

Questions? We Have Answers.

ElderShield works with any standard Indian SIM card — Airtel, Jio, Vi (Vodafone-Idea), and BSNL. You need a basic prepaid SIM with SMS activated. No data plan or smartphone required. A simple ₹19 top-up with SMS validity is all you need to run the device.

Absolutely. ElderShield uses the SIM800L GSM cellular module to send SMS messages — the same technology that basic phones have used for decades. As long as there is any cellular signal in the area, the device will send the alert. No Wi-Fi, no internet data, no smartphone needed.

Our two-stage algorithm makes false alarms extremely rare. The device first detects a sharp impact (above 2.5g), then waits 500ms and checks if the person is lying still. Everyday activities like sitting down fast, jumping, or bumping the device will NOT trigger an alert. In 3 months of customer data, false alarm rates are under 2%.

Setup takes under 15 minutes and every device comes with a printed Quick Start Guide. We also provide free video call setup support — one of our team members will walk you through inserting the SIM card, registering your emergency numbers, and testing the device. Most customers are up and running within 20 minutes of opening the box.

The 18650 Li-ion battery provides approximately 6–7 hours of continuous monitoring on a full charge. Charging via the USB-C port takes about 2–3 hours. The recommended routine is to charge it overnight (like a phone) and clip it on in the morning. The TP4056 module has built-in overcharge protection for battery safety.
